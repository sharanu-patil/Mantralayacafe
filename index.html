Our Story

Food cooked with devotion, served with warmth

Named in honour of the sacred town of Mantralayam — abode of Sri Raghavendra Swamy — our cafe carries forward a tradition of sattvic cooking that feeds the soul as much as the body. No onion, no garlic, just pure, carefully spiced South Indian food made the old way.


From piping-hot idlis at sunrise to crispy dosas for lunch and warm rasam rice in the evenings, every item on our menu is a nod to the kitchens of Karnataka and Andhra. Walk in hungry. Leave at peace.
